DST-ICE-FLINGOMATIC-ENHANCED

Expands Ice Flingomatic coverage so it can protect a larger base area.

中文

当前功能：
- 雪球发射器的范围从原版 `15` 提升到 `20`。
- 更大的范围会同时覆盖探火、灭火、警报和紧急模式。
- 每次灭火触发都会补射 `3` 发雪球，形成更密集的短间隔连射。
- 更适合保护面积更大的基地。

补充说明：
- 目前会改范围和单次灭火时的雪球密度，但不会额外增加燃料消耗。
- 已修正一次手持摆放预览和落地后实际范围不一致的问题。
- 已修正一次增强范围最外侧目标偶尔灭不到的问题。
- 当前版本没有做外部配置文件开关。

English

Current features:
- Ice Flingomatic range is increased from the vanilla `15` to `20`.
- The larger radius also applies to fire detection, extinguishing, warning coverage, and emergency mode.
- Each extinguish trigger also adds `3` extra snowballs for a denser short burst.
- Better suited for protecting larger bases.

Notes:
- This mod now changes range and per-trigger snowball density, without extra fuel cost.
- A previous mismatch between the placement preview and the deployed live range has been fixed.
- A previous issue affecting extinguish reliability at the outer edge of the enhanced radius has also been fixed.
- There are no external configuration options in this version.
